About Us

We revolutionize humanity's future by fusing human and artificial intelligence to solve our world's greatest challenges.

It is with great honor and profound enthusiasm that I announce the official establishment of the Institute for Advanced Intelligence Study, having received formal approval from the Korean Government.

As the director of this esteemed institute, I extend an invitation for academic partnership, recognizing the critical importance of intelligence in human progress. Our fundamental mission is to explore the essence of human intelligence—the very cornerstone of human history and culture—and to harness its potential through the transformative power of advanced artificial intelligence technologies.

We are committed to advancing human knowledge across multiple domains, believing that the convergence of human insight and technological innovation will propel mankind toward unprecedented achievements. The institute stands as a beacon of intellectual exploration, dedicated to understanding and expanding the boundaries of intelligence.

I am deeply convinced that collaborative academic endeavors will be instrumental in realizing our ambitious objectives. Research and development will be the pillars of our institute, driving meaningful progress and opening new frontiers of understanding.

We look forward to the opportunity to meet in person, to establish a robust foundation for high-quality research that will shape the future of human potential. Our vision is clear: to illuminate the path forward through rigorous scholarship and innovative thinking.

The future is bright, and together, we shall forge it.

President. Dr. Jee Hang Lee (Ph.D. in Multi-Agent Systems, Post-doc in Neuroscience)
